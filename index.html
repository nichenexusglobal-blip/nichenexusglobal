How it works
01

Trade has a size problem

Chinese manufacturers need volume. Small and medium buyers pay inflated prices because the system wasn't built for them. We change that — using technology to replace what used to require personal networks and years of experience.

The Big Buyer Advantage

Factories give their best prices to buyers who order by the container. If you're not one of them, you pay more — sometimes double — for the same product from the same factory.

The Middleman Tax

Trade intermediaries add 15-30% on top of factory prices. They exist because small buyers can't go direct — not because the work is worth that much. We remove the layer.

AI as the Equalizer

We use AI to do what used to need relationships, travel, and years of market knowledge — finding the right factory, verifying capability, negotiating pricing. The cost drops, the access opens.
