# Key Business Learnings

## Product Strategy
- Our competitive advantage: $0.15-0.29/Wh LiFePO4 power stations vs EcoFlow ~$0.5-1.0/Wh
- Best target customers: existing EcoFlow/Bluetti dealers who want a lower-cost complementary line
- MOQ: 50-100 units for most factories, 30-35 day production lead time
- Private label/OEM available from most suppliers

## Market Prioritization
1. Nigeria - M Million already replied, Alaba Market distribution
2. Kenya - Active distributor network (Batteriq, Dong Solar, Axcellis)
3. South Africa - B2B distributors looking for lines
4. Philippines - Multi-brand distributors (Prime Tech, Bluetti PH)
5. Indonesia - Growing solar market
6. USA - Pet GPS collars (SpotOn, PetSafe, SkyShepherd as OEM partners)

## Customer Development Process
1. Search → find potential distributor/dealer
2. Verify → visit website, confirm business exists
3. Research → understand their product mix, brands, market position
4. Find contact → email/WhatsApp from website footer
5. Draft → write unique message referencing their specific business
6. Gate → pass precision gate (95 email / 70 WhatsApp)
7. Submit to Pen → get approval before send
8. Send → universal_send_gate handles deliver
9. Track → auto-update bullet status
